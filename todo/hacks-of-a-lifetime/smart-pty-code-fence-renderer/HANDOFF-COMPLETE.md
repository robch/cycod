## Reflection: What Was Learned About Handoffs

### Good Handoff Practices Demonstrated

1. **Start with orientation** - Where are we?
2. **Recap recent work** - What just happened?
3. **Provide options** - What could we do next?
4. **Recommend strongly** - What should we do?
5. **Detail the steps** - How do we do it?
6. **Give context** - Why this way?
7. **Include examples** - Show, don't just tell
8. **State confidence** - How sure are we?

### What Makes Handoffs Work

- **Completeness** - Answer all likely questions
- **Clarity** - No ambiguity in guidance
- **Actionability** - Specific next actions
- **Honesty** - Admit diminishing returns
- **Respect** - Don't waste future-AI's time

### What This Enables

- Self-perpetuating work sessions
- No context loss between sessions
- Clear decision making
- Efficient progress
- High confidence
